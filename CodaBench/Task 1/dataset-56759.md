<h1>Data Description</h1>
<p>The dataset for this task, <strong>Multi-Dialect-Sent (MDS-3)</strong>, consists of 3,000 sentences annotated for 3-class sentiment (positive, negative, neutral).</p>
<p>The dataset is balanced across five major dialects:
<ul>
    <li>Moroccan</li>
    <li>Egyptian</li>
    <li>Jordanian</li>
    <li>Saudi</li>
</ul>
</p>
<p>The data was sourced from Hotel reviews, then translated and annotated by native speakers of each dialect.</p>
